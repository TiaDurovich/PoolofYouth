About

Pool of Youth

We are a team of three Year 13 high school students (Tia, Jeune and Kahn) from Marist College, Auckland, alongside our Digital Technology teacher, Mr Paterson. Us students have been exploring the vastness of the technological world together since 2020, and this year grew a great passion for blockchain.

Collectively, we are interested in how we can leverage the Cardano blockchain in order to spark positive change, and we enjoy learning about current projects being developed by Cardano-enthusiasts across the globe—especially those driven by youth!
