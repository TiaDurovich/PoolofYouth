Our Vision

Founded by a group of high school students from New Zealand, Pool of Youth is a collective focused on promoting young people in blockchain. Our purpose is to elevate Cardano blockchain projects developed by students in high school by providing a space where individuals can have their work and profile displayed. Furthermore, we wish to allow youth to connect with other likeminded individuals, developing a Cardano network for young people across the globe.
